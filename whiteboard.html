Try Whiteboard

The whiteboard feature on our peer learning edtech website is an incredibly powerful tool for students and teachers alike. It allows for a dynamic, interactive learning experience that can be used for a wide range of purposes, from explaining complex concepts to collaborative problem-solving. The whiteboard feature comes with a range of tools that make it easy for users to express their ideas and collaborate effectively. Users can use a pen tool to draw or write freehand, which is great for explaining concepts, annotating diagrams, and working out mathematical equations. A highlighter tool is also available to emphasize key points and make them stand out. In addition to the pen and highlighter tools, users can also access a range of shapes and symbols that can be added to the whiteboard. This includes standard shapes like squares, circles, and triangles, as well as specialized symbols like arrows, flowcharts, and graphs. These tools are particularly useful when working on technical diagrams or engineering projects.
