Try Screen Sharing

The screen sharing feature on our peer learning edtech website is a powerful tool that allows users to share their screens with others in real-time. This feature is particularly useful for remote learning, where students and teachers may not be in the same physical location. It enables users to collaborate effectively and work together on projects, assignments, and other learning materials. The screen sharing feature on our edtech website is easy to use and comes with a range of tools that make it a versatile tool for sharing information. Users can share their entire screen or select specific applications or documents to share. This is particularly useful when working on a project with others, as it allows users to control what is being shared and keep the focus on the task at hand.
